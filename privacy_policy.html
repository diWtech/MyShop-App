Privacy policy

Effective Date: August 01, 2025

This Privacy Policy has been created to help you understand how your information is handled while using our application. Please read it carefully to gain a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect, or otherwise manage your information.

My Shop is a mobile Point of Sale (POS) application designed to help shop owners, small businesses, and individuals manage their products and sales directly from their Android devices. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

We, the developers of My Shop, do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data. All the information you enter in the app is stored locally on your device only. The types of data you may choose to enter include:

  • Product details (product code, product name, category, quantity, buy price, sell price, manufacture date, expiry date)
  • Sales records (items sold, sales amount, payment method, amount paid, change, and additional notes)
  • Locally stored identifiers you create within the app (such as product codes or custom notes)
  • Advertising identifiers (used by Google AdMob to display ads — no personally identifiable information is shared by us)
  • General usage data collected automatically by the Google Play Store or AdMob (for example: device type, crash logs, performance data)

2. How Information Is Used

All personal data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not send, transmit, or back up this information to any remote server or cloud service. As developers, we do not have access to any of your personal or sales-related data.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You can edit or delete any data within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

All information is stored securely on your local device storage. You are solely responsible for managing your data, including backing it up or deleting it. Please be aware that if your device is lost or the data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

5. Third-Party Services

My Shop app uses third-party services to provide certain features. These include:

  • Advertising platforms (e.g., Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display banner and other ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personally identifiable information (such as device identifiers, usage data, and general location) in order to provide personalized and non-personalized advertisements. For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not share any personal or sales-related data with Google AdMob or any other third party. Any information collected for advertising purposes is collected directly by Google.

While the app itself does not collect or transmit your personal data, please note that Google Play may automatically collect basic information such as device type, app usage statistics, and crash reports. This information is managed by Google under their own Privacy Policy and is not accessible to the app developer.

6. GDPR Compliance (EEA & UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may be shown a consent form when using the app. This consent form allows you to choose whether you would like to receive personalized or non-personalized ads. You can update or withdraw your consent at any time via your device or Google account settings.

7. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a resident of California, you have the right to opt out of personalized advertising. You can exercise this right by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children.
